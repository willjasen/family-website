Burial: PFC. Billie Faust Hicks

Text
Pfc. Billie F. Hicks

The body of Pfc. Billie Foust Hicks, 19, will arrive Tuesday at 2:15 p.m., at the Clinchfield Railroad Station and will be removed to Hamlett-Dobson Chapel. Funeral services are tentatively scheduled Wednesday at 2:30 p.m., at Reedy Creek Presbyterian Church at Orebank. The Rev. Martin Perry will be in charge. Burial will be in Gunnings Cemetary.
Pfc. Hicks, a resident of Orrbank, was killed in action April 3, 1944, in Italy. He attended school at Orebank and Blountville High School and left for the Army on April 8, 1943.
Surviving are his parents, Mr. and Mrs. W. B. Hicks; and four sisters, Miss Mary Ella Hicks, Miss Ruth Hicks, Miss Trula and Miss Peggie Hicks, all of Orebank; and his grandfather. James Kerns of Orebank.
A military funeral will be held at the grave by the American Legion. Pallbearers will be Ed Hicks, John N. Hicks, W. F. Hicks, Charlie Hurst, Ike McGaffey, and Hicks Foulk.
