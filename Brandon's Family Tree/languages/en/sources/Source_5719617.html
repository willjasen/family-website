Text
At approximately 2:30 a.m.. Sgt. Wayne Anderson was coming out of Food City Grocery, Stone Drive and observed a truck exit from behind the Leader Store sale tent headed toward Stone Drive with the lights off and traveling at an excessive speed. Anderson radioed for assistance. Officers Gale Osborne and Gibson chased the vehicle. After the truck continued on Stone Drive and turned off on New Bluff Road, the driver of the truck rammed the side of Osborne's police car. The police finally got the truck that was full of clothes, stopped in the Fox Fire Subdivision at the radio tower. Officer Givens checked the Leader Store tent sales and there had been a burglary. Arrested and charged with burglary, assault with an auto, and resisting arrest were Luke L. Minor, 32, 5733/ Lehigh St., Route 18, and Martin Lynn Begley, 24, 316 Archcrest St. Recovered was $1,993.55 worth of clothes. Reported at 3:02 a.m.

Arrested for possession of stolen property from The Leader tent sale, Food City #11, East Stone Drive,
Eckerd Drugs, Lynn Garden Drive, Lay's Store, World of Clothing, and Revco, King's Giant Plaza, were Gordon Gross, 25, Helen Shepherd, 33, Carlos R. Monroe, 22, Shirley I. Doty, 24, and Nancy B. Hall, 26. All were from Neon, Ky., and were charged with possession of stolen property valued at $100. Reported at 5:33 p.m.
