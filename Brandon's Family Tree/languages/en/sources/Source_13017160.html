Title Taylor's Meeting House
State/Territory Tennessee

Entries assigned to this source

Text
The Presbyterian Church established here in 1773 is probably the first church of any denomniation to be established within the borders of Tennessee. It was also used as a fort, and school was held here on Sundays, in pioneer times.
