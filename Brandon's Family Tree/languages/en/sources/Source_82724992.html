PFC. John "Buster" Edward Deel

Text
FURLOUGH
Pfc. John (Buster) Deel, son of Mr. and Mrs. M. M. Deel, Orebank, has returend to the hospital at Camp Cambell, Ky., after spending a 30-day leave withhis parents. Pfc. Deel received the Purple Heart for wounds received November 26 in Korea. He was taken to a hospital in Tokyo, Japan, and later flown to the United States. Before enlisting in the Army in March, 1950, he was employed by Black Plumbing and Heating Company. He is a former student of Cleveland High School, Cleveland, Va.
