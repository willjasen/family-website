Notes
Walter Buchanan 11th Chief of Clan Buchanan was born in 1338, in Stirlingshire, Scotland as the son of Maurice Buchanan and Margaret Menteith. He married Margaret Beauchamp. They were the parents of at least 2 sons and 2 daughters. He died in 1420, in Stirling, Stirlingshire, Scotland, at the age of 82.
