Notes
When James Henry Lane was born in April 1865, in Scott, Virginia, United States, his father, Joseph T. Lane, was 52 and his mother, Mary A. Polly Lane, was 33. He married Mary E. Owens on 10 February 1886, in Scott, Virginia, United States. They were the parents of at least 3 sons and 4 daughters. He lived in Banister, Pittsylvania, Virginia, United States in 1900 and DeKalb District, Scott, Virginia, United States for about 10 years. He died on 26 December 1924, in Scott, Virginia, United States, at the age of 59, and was buried in Brickey Cemetery, Scott, Virginia, United States.
