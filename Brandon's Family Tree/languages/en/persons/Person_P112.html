George Buchanan, son of John, was born in 1580 and became the Second Laird of
Gartincaber and the First Laird of Blairlusk. He married Elizabeth Leckie and had four sons,
John, George, Thomas, and Andrew. (WB, p. 239; IM, p. 31)
