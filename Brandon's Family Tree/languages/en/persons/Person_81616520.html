Notes
When talking with Peggy at her house on 5/14/2025, she wished for her name to be stylized as "Peggy Ann Hicks (Parker, Moore)" with attention to the sorting of the married names as she wanted Parker to be first.
On 5/17/2025, I talked with Peggy for about 30 minutes to update her on what I had found out so far since meeting with her on 5/14/2025:

* Billie’s girlfriend is Lola R. Robinette (Nichols); she passed away in 2016
* I found a few more pictures of Lindy; her full name is Martha Melinda Kern (Hobbs) and she passed away at 95 years old
* I have found a style difference for the Kern/Kerns name as some early documents have the name as Kerns but the gravestones have Kern
* Velva was likely a neighbor to Patrick Monroe Begley in Fort Blackmore, VA (my mom’s dad’s dad)
* I found a picture of John Lewis Kern, father to James Oscan Kern (granny’s father) and father to Lindy (Martha Melinda Kern-Hobbs)
* Monnie’s death certificate says she was buried in Reedy Creek (but that’s vague)
* If I had to guess where Monnie is buried, I would choose Arcadia Church Cemetery because her 2nd husband who is the father to the three children including Granny Velva is buried there with his 2nd wife. There are old very old gravestones there and some are completely unreadable.
