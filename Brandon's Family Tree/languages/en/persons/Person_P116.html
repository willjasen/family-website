Notes
When Thomas Buchanan 3rd of Carbeth was born on 30 August 1522, in Buchanan Castle, Stirlingshire, Scotland, United Kingdom, his father, Thomas Buchanan 2nd Laird of Carbeth, was 33 and his mother, Margaret Edmonstone, was 47. He married Elisabeth Wallis Livingstone about 1535, in Scotland, United Kingdom. They were the parents of at least 1 son. He died on 20 February 1582, in Buchanan Castle, Stirlingshire, Scotland, at the age of 59.
