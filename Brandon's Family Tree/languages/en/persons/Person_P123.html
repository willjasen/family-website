Notes
When Isabella Stewart was born about 1398, in The Lennox, Dunbartonshire, Scotland, her father, Sir Murdoch Stewart 2nd Duke of Albany, was 37 and her mother, Isabella Countess of Lennox, Duchess of Albany, was 26. She had at least 4 sons and 2 daughters with Walter Buchanan 13th Chief of Buchanan. She died on 13 October 1494, in her hometown, at the age of 97, and was buried in Diocese of Vannes, France.
