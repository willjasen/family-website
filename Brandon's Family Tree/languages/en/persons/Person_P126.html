♂︎ John ❺, who married about 1392, Janet de Lany, the heiress of Leny and was the ancestor of the cadets of Leny

[Buchanan of Auchmar identifies this John as the 12th Chief; but was known as the 1st of Leny]
Sir John Buchanan, son of Walter, Twelfth Laird of Buchanan, is mentioned in a charter of
King Robert III in 1363. He married Janet Lenny. John died before his father and had at least two
sons: Sir Alexander, who killed the English Duke of Clarence in the Battle of Baugé in 1420 but
died without an heir, and Walter, who succeeded his father. (WB, pp. 170-171)
