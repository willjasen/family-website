Notes
When Thomas Buchanan 2nd Laird of Carbeth was born about 1490, in Carbeth, Stirlingshire, Scotland, United Kingdom, his father, Patrick Buchanan 16th Chief of Buchanan, was 26 and his mother, Margaret Campbell, was 15. He married Margaret Edmonstone in 1522, in Stirlingshire, Scotland, United Kingdom. They were the parents of at least 1 son and 1 daughter. He died on 5 December 1590, in Stirlingshire, Scotland, United Kingdom, at the age of 101, and was buried in Edinburgh, Scotland.
