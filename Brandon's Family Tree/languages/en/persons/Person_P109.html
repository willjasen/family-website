Notes
When Jean Agnes Livingston was born in 1629, in Dumbarton, Dunbartonshire, Scotland, her father, Alexander Livingston 2nd Earl of Linlithgow, was 54 and her mother, Mary Douglas, was 32. She married John Buchanan of Blairlusk on 22 May 1649, in Buchanan, Stirlingshire, Scotland, United Kingdom. They were the parents of at least 5 sons and 4 daughters. She died about March 1656, in Dunbartonshire, Scotland, at the age of 27, and was buried in Dunfermline, Fife, Scotland, United Kingdom.
