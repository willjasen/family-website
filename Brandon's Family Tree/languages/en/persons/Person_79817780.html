Notes
Joseph was an immigrant from Ireland and came over on a ship. The ship that came here docked in Savannah, GA.
When Joseph T. Lane was born in 1813, in Scott, Virginia, United States, his father, William Aaron Lane Sr, was 40 and his mother, Barbara Barnes, was 35. He married Polly Mccarey before 1834. They were the parents of at least 2 sons and 2 daughters. He lived in Tennessee, United States in 1870 and DeKalb District, Scott, Virginia, United States in 1870. He died in Scott, Virginia, United States.
