Notes
MacBeath McAuslan - 6th Laird of Clan Buchanan was born in 1165, in Buchanan, Stirlingshire, Scotland as the son of McAuslan. He had at least 1 son with Lady Margaret MacMillans. He died on 23 December 1240, in his hometown, at the age of 75, and was buried in Buchanan, Stirlingshire, Scotland.
