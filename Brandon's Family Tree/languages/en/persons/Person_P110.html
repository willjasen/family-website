When John Buchanan of Blairlusk was born on 1 October 1620, in Stirlingshire, Scotland, United Kingdom, his father, Sir George Buchanan, was 40 and his mother, Lady Elizabeth Preston, was 25. He married Jean Agnes Livingston on 22 May 1649, in Buchanan, Stirlingshire, Scotland, United Kingdom. They were the parents of at least 5 sons and 4 daughters. He died in December 1682, in Dunbartonshire, Scotland, at the age of 62, and was buried in Dunfermline, Fife, Scotland.
John Buchanan, son of George and Elizabeth, was the Second Laird of Blairlusk. He was one
of the many Buchanans who joined the Coventeers and renounced the Catholic church. He
married Jean Buchanan or Livingston, and they had two sons, George and William. He died in
1662. (WB, p. 239; IM, pp. 31, 39)
