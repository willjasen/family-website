Notes
Thomas Buchanan, son of Walter. He was given the lands of Gartincaber near Auchmar in
1461 and became the First Laird of Carbeth in 1476. He had two sons, Thomas and John.
Thomas became the Second Laird of Carbeth in 1493, but died without children. His title passed
to his nephew, the son of his brother John, whose name was also Thomas. (WB, p. 235)
